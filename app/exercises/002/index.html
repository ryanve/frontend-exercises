Neighborhood Picnic

Kennydale’s Big Community Event of the Year!

City of Renton Officials and Staff Members provide and expertly grill the hot dogs

To ensure that there are plenty of food choices for all, we always ask all attendees to bring a potluck item to share:

  • Last name A to M: please bring a dessert
  • Last name N to Z: please bring a salad or side dish

Oh yeah! Fun! Fun! Fun!

This free, fun filled event will also include games and activities for the kids, including:

  • Face painting
  • Door Prizes for members
  • Displays by the Renton Fire, Police, EMT’s
  • The King County Mobile “Library2GO” Van
  • and MORE!!

To help ensure your comfort, it’s a good idea to bring a blanket or chairs for sitting – and no pets please.

We’ll see you there next September!
