What to do?

Here is what you'll be doing for each task

  1. Read the instructions
  2. Modify the CSS, HTML and/or JavaScript
  3. In making changes, keep **detailed notes** of your thought process, we will like to hear about it
  4. Save your work
  5. Share your work by pushing to Github (or by emailing your ontact).

There are no wrong answers.
